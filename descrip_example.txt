Kim Adler

Born: 1967 in Springfield, Massachusetts
16 Professional Women’s Bowlers Association titles.
In 1999, she won the U.S. Women’s Open.
She was inducted into the United States Bowling Congress Hall of Fame in 2016.

Earl Anthony

Born: 1938 in Tacoma, Washington
43 Professional Bowlers Association titles.
In 2008, he was voted the greatest bowler in Professional Bowler Association history.
He was inducted into the United States Bowling Congress Hall of Fame in 1986.
